For the main keyword search — "plumber in Knoxville Tennessee" — SMK Plumbing & Electric ranks at approximately position 12. SMK Plumbing & Electric Google Business Profile Review

SMK Plumbing & Electric has 233 reviews with a perfect 5-star rating. The review velocity looks healthy — they have a steady flow of new reviews coming in, which is more important for rankings than the total count. Google weights recent, consistent reviews higher than a large batch from a year ago. SMK Plumbing & Electric appears to have a working review system and should keep doing what they are doing.

Their photos are diverse and show different types of projects and team members. None are stock images. Geotagging photos with GPS coordinates from specific target areas like Sequoyah Hills — the wealthier part of Knoxville — could help reinforce the business's relevance in those neighborhoods. The photo situation is solid overall.

One area for improvement is their GBP categories. SMK Plumbing & Electric has seven categories set. As a general rule, we recommend one primary category at most three. Too many categories can confuse Google's algorithm about what the business's core service actually is, diluting the ranking power of the primary keyword. We would recommend pruning down to Plumber as the primary category with one or two supporting categories, and moving everything else into the services section.

SMK Plumbing & Electric does have services listed, which puts them ahead of most competitors. The next step is adding descriptions to the top 20 services to capture broader search queries from people looking for specific types of plumbing work in Knoxville.

The booking link is set up but currently does not land on their actual contact form — it goes to a different page. A quick fix would be to update the booking URL to point directly to the contact page with the inquiry form, reducing friction for potential customers trying to reach them.

The Q&A / FAQ section should also be populated with 5–10 common customer questions. This gives Google more keyword-rich content directly on the profile and answers customer questions before they even have to ask.

SMK Plumbing & Electric Website SEO Review

The SMK Plumbing & Electric website title tag reads "Knoxville Plumbing and Electric SMK Plumbing and Electric." This includes the keyword, location, and business name, which is exactly what we want to see. No changes needed here.

The H1 tag contains the keyword and city, which is correct, though there appears to be a display or interface issue on the website that is affecting how it renders visually. If SMK Plumbing & Electric wants their website to look as professional as possible, that visual glitch should be fixed.

A significant finding is that SMK Plumbing & Electric does not have their address displayed anywhere on their website. On their GBP, the address is listed as 3300 Rifle Range Road, with Road abbreviated as "RD." Since the address does not appear on the website at all, Google is missing a key NAP signal from one of the most important platforms. We would recommend adding the full address to the website footer using the exact same formatting as the GBP — including "RD" not "Road" — and embedding a Google Map alongside it. The map sends Google another location confirmation signal and gives visitors an easy way to understand where the business operates.

NAP consistency matters because when Google sees mismatched or missing business information across platforms, it creates uncertainty about which version is correct. That uncertainty directly costs rankings. The fix is simple — put the address on the website — but it needs to match the GBP exactly.

SMK Plumbing & Electric has 27 indexed pages, which is close to our minimum target of 30. The content includes service-specific pages like "Knoxville septic services" that allow the business to compete for specific search queries. Adding a few more service or area pages would push them past the 30-page threshold and create more opportunities to capture search traffic.

SMK Plumbing & Electric Technical SEO

Website speed has room for improvement. Google has confirmed that page speed directly influences rankings, and slow sites lose visitors. If a potential customer taps a link and stares at a loading screen for four seconds, they hit back and call a competitor. We would look at the backend code to identify what is causing the speed issues and fix them.

The site has a valid SSL certificate (HTTPS).

SMK Plumbing & Electric does not have LocalBusiness schema markup on their website. Schema is a structured data format that packages the business's name, address, phone number, hours, and services in a way Google can read instantly. Adding it takes 15–20 minutes and removes any ambiguity about who the business is and where they operate. This would be one of the first things we would fix.
