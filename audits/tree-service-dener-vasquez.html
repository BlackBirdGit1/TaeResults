Tree Service Dener Vasquez Google Business Profile Review

Tree Service Dener Vasquez has only 9 Google reviews. Having audited many tree service businesses in Knoxville, this is on the very lower end of the market. However, the total number is not the most critical factor — velocity is. Google weights consistent, recent reviews more heavily than total count. A business with 30 reviews that are all from the past two months will outrank a business with 200 reviews that stopped coming in a year ago.

For Tree Service Dener Vasquez, the priority should be building a review generation system that produces at least 2–3 new reviews per week. This means sending a follow-up text or email with a direct review link after every completed job. A steady stream of recent reviews signals to Google that the business is active and trusted, which directly improves rankings.

Their photos need attention. While the photos are real, there are duplicate images in the gallery. Google recognizes duplicate photos, and having too many can risk being flagged as spam. For an example of strong photo diversity done right, see our L&M Tree Service audit — their photos are a genuine strength. Tree Service Dener Vasquez should remove duplicates and add more diverse photo types — completed projects, before-and-after shots, crew members at work, equipment. Making it a routine to add new photos regularly and geotagging each one with GPS coordinates from specific job sites will help Google understand where the business operates and strengthen relevance for searches in those areas.

Their GBP category is set to just Tree Service, which is exactly right. We recommend one primary category at most three to avoid confusing Google's algorithm about what the business does. Tree Service Dener Vasquez has this correct.

The services section is empty. Google allows up to 99 services, and we recommend loading at least 30 with descriptions for the top 20. This captures the long-tail search queries that categories alone cannot reach — things like "tree trimming Knoxville" or "stump removal near me." Without services listed, Tree Service Dener Vasquez is missing those specific searches entirely.

The overall GBP profile completeness needs work. The profile does not appear to be fully completed — the completion indicator is not green. Missing items likely include hours, a full description, attributes, a booking link, and Q&A / FAQ entries. Every empty field reduces Google's confidence in the listing. Taking 20 minutes to answer all of Google's setup questions is one of the simplest things Tree Service Dener Vasquez can do to improve rankings immediately.

Tree Service Dener Vasquez Indexing Issue

This is one of the most important findings in the audit. Tree Service Dener Vasquez has service pages and area pages built on their website — content that should be helping them rank for specific searches. However, when we ran a site: search in Google, only 4 pages are showing as indexed. The rest of their content is invisible to Google. We recommend having at least 30 indexed pages.

This means Tree Service Dener Vasquez has done the content work but is not getting credit for it. Someone searching "tree cutting service Tennessee" right now would not find their dedicated tree cutting page because Google has not indexed it. This is likely a Google Search Console or technical SEO issue. We found a similar indexing problem in our Sunrise Plumbing and Remodel LLC audit where pages existed on the website but were invisible to Google. — possibly missing from the sitemap, blocked by robots.txt, or having noindex tags. Diagnosing and fixing whatever is preventing these pages from being indexed would immediately unlock ranking potential that is currently being wasted.

Tree Service Dener Vasquez Technical SEO

The website performance score is very low. Page speed is a confirmed Google ranking factor, and slow sites lose visitors. Common causes include uncompressed images, heavy scripts, and content loading issues. Improving the backend code to speed up the site would directly influence rankings and reduce bounce rate — every second of loading time costs potential customers.

The site has a valid SSL certificate (HTTPS).

Tree Service Dener Vasquez does have LocalBusiness schema markup implemented, which is a strong technical signal. There is a minor error with the service type field that should be corrected, but having schema in place at all puts them ahead of most competitors.
