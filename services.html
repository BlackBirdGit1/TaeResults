Phase 1 · Week 1

Benchmarking & Quick-Win Audit

I run a grid-based ranking heatmap on your main keyword, analyze your top 10 competitors (reviews, photos, services, categories, indexed pages), and identify every low-hanging-fruit fix — NAP inconsistencies, missing schema, missing Google Map embed, page speed issues, and more. This becomes the baseline we measure all progress against.

Phase 2 · Weeks 2-4

Google Business Profile Optimization

I prune your categories to one primary, load up to 99 services with descriptions for the top 20, set up bookings, add 5-10 FAQ entries, and fill out every single field Google offers. I also build a review acquisition system — direct link, QR code, follow-up template — so you consistently get 2-3 new reviews per week on autopilot. This is not advice to "get more reviews." It is a system.

Phase 3 · Week 5

On-Page SEO

I optimize your title tags (200-character format, keyword + city + business name), header structure (H1 with primary keyword + city, H2s for services), hard-code your NAP in the footer, embed Google Maps, and check Core Web Vitals — mobile speed needs to be 70 or above. If you do not have schema markup, I add LocalBusiness schema. If your site is not on HTTPS, I fix it. Every ranking signal Google has confirmed — I check it and fix it.

Phase 4 · Weeks 6-8

Content & Local Authority

I build out service pages (1,500-2,500 words each) and area pages for 5+ neighborhoods and towns you serve. Each page is structured with local hooks, FAQs, internal links, and a clear call to action. This proves to Google that you are the expert in your service and your area. I also set up interlinking between service pages and area pages so Google can crawl and understand your entire site structure.

Phase 5 · Weeks 9-10

Citations & Backlinks

Before building new citations, I audit your existing ones for NAP inconsistencies — old addresses, wrong phone numbers, misspelled street names. Then I build citations across 50+ directories: Apple Business Connect, Bing Places, Yelp, Facebook, Nextdoor, Foursquare, MapQuest, industry-specific directories, and even automotive navigation platforms. I also pursue local backlinks — Chamber of Commerce, local event sponsorships, and complementary business partnerships.

Phase 6 · Week 11

Competitor Cleanup

If fake or spam listings are blocking you from the top 3, I identify and report them. Keyword-stuffed business names, fake addresses, stock-photo-only profiles — I verify they are illegitimate and submit reports with evidence. This only happens after every other optimization is in place.

Ongoing · Week 12+

Weekly Maintenance & Reporting

Every week I publish a GBP update post, upload geotagged photos, manage review responses, and monitor Google Search Console for crawl errors and ranking shifts. Monthly, I review content for pages that could be pushed from page 2 to page 1, update outdated information, and send you a ranking report showing exactly how your position has changed since we started.
